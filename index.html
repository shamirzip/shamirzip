What is Secret Sharing?

This tool uses Shamir's Secret Sharing to split sensitive information (like wallet backups, passwords, or private keys) into multiple parts called "shares". You can store each share in a different location for security.

How it works:
  • Split: Your secret is compressed and divided into shares. For example, with a "2-of-3" setup, you create 3 shares and need any 2 to recover your secret.
  • Combine: Enter the required number of shares to reconstruct your original secret.
  • QR Codes: Generate QR codes for each share to print and store physically.
Common Uses:
  • Backup Bitcoin wallet configurations (like multisig setups)
  • Split master passwords across multiple locations
  • Create family recovery systems where multiple people must collaborate
💡 Pro Tip: Use "2-of-3" or "3-of-5" setups. This way, you can lose one share and still recover your secret, but no single person or location has access.
⚠️ Important: This app works entirely offline in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. Always test recovery before relying on it for critical data.
